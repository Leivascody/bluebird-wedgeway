Executive Summary

An institutional-quality industrial asset acquired below replacement cost.

Bluebird Commercial Real Estate is acquiring 4061 Wedgeway Court (Suites 4061–4073) — a 43,426 SF multi-tenant industrial property in Earth City, Missouri — for a $3.6M purchase price ($82.90 PSF), meaningfully below replacement cost. The asset is 100% leased to four established tenants with staggered expirations, providing durable cash flow with built-in mark-to-market upside as in-place rents reset toward market over the 10-year hold.

Investment Highlights

Why Wedgeway

Four reasons this asset offers compelling risk-adjusted returns for accredited investors and family offices.

Below Replacement Cost

Acquisition basis of $82.90 PSF — meaningfully below the $130–150 PSF cost to construct comparable infill industrial today.

100% Leased, Staggered

Four tenants with no single expiration exceeding 35% of the rent roll, mitigating rollover and concentration risk.

Mark-to-Market Upside

In-place rents trail submarket comps by roughly 18–25%, creating organic NOI growth as leases roll through the hold.

Earth City Submarket

St. Louis County's premier infill industrial corridor — direct I-70 access, sub-4% vacancy, and exceptional logistics fundamentals.

Transaction Timeline

From LOI to Closing

A disciplined six-phase execution plan from market identification through stabilization.

Q4 2025

Market Identification & LOI

Sourced off-market via Summit's Earth City broker network. Initial LOI submitted with 30-day exclusivity and pricing tied to in-place NOI.

Q1 2026

Due Diligence

60-day diligence period covering Phase I ESA, PCA, structural review, tenant interviews, and full lease abstraction.

Q1 2026

Loan Underwriting

Senior debt sized at 70% LTV with Midwest BankCentre. Rate locked at 6.15% on a 5-year fixed term with 6 months of I/O.

Q2 2026

Equity Raise & Closing

$1.24M equity raise from accredited investors and family offices. Targeting close on or before June 30, 2026.

Q2–Q4 2026

Capex & Lease Strategy

Execute targeted roof, HVAC, and parking lot capex. Begin lease renewal discussions with the 2026 expiration well in advance.

2027–2036

Hold & Exit

Quarterly investor reporting throughout the 10-year hold, with a mid-hold refinance at Year 5. Targeted exit at a 7.25% terminal cap — projected 14.2% LP IRR and 3.32x equity multiple.
